        How It Works

        1️⃣ Upload Resume

        Drag and drop or browse to upload single or multiple resumes for analysis.

        2️⃣ Text Extraction

        The system extracts text from PDFs or DOCX files and cleans it for processing.

        3️⃣ NLP Analysis

        Applies tokenization, vectorization, and keyword extraction to understand the content.

        4️⃣ ML Classification

        Uses trained machine learning models to predict the most suitable job role for each resume.

        5️⃣ ATS & Job Match

        Calculates ATS score and determines if the resume matches the predicted role.

        6️⃣ Suggestions

        Provides personalized recommendations to improve your resume for future submissions.
